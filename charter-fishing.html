ℹ️ About Us

Bottomed Out Fishing Charters is your trusted name in Gulf Shores deep sea fishing. Captain Ken Tompkins brings years of experience on the Gulf of Mexico, running trips out of Tacky Jacks Fort Morgan on a 30' NauticStar Center Console.

Perfect For: Families, bachelor parties, corporate outings, experienced anglers, or first-timers looking for an unforgettable day on the water.

✨ What’s Included
✅ Professional rods & reels
✅ Live bait & lures
✅ Fishing license
✅ Fish cleaning & filleting
✅ First Mate assistance
✅ Ice & fish bags
✅ Safety equipment
✅ Up to 6 guests
